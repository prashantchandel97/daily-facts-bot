event_summary: Ian Fleming, creator of James Bond, was born on May 28, 1908; he based Bond partly on himself, gave the character his own Jamaican retreat as a base, and chose the name "James Bond" from the cover of a bird-watching book on his shelf because it sounded as dull and ordinary as possible.
